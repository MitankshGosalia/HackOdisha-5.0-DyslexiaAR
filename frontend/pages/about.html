Our mission

We build tools that make reading clearer, kinder, and more inclusive.

Why we built this

Millions of people live with dyslexia. Small design choices can have an outsized impact on comfort and comprehension. We created DyslexiaAR to bring assistive typography into the real world—classrooms, offices, streets—using a camera you already have.
